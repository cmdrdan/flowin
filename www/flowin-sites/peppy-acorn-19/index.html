History of TVR Cars

TVR is a British manufacturer of high-end sports cars. The company was founded in 1947 and has produced some iconic vehicles over the years.

TVR T350

The TVR T350 is a sports car produced by TVR from 2002 to 2006. It is powered by a 3.6L inline-six engine and is known for its lightweight construction and impressive performance.

Cool Facts about TVR T350:

  • The T350 was available in both coupe and targa versions.
  • It featured a distinctive design with sharp angles and aggressive styling.
  • With a top speed of over 170 mph, the T350 was one of the fastest cars in its class.
